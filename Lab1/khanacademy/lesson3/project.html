Ramen Buldak

  • Time: 20 min
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Ramen noodles 2 packs
Buldak spicy sauce 4 tablespoons
Cheese (Mozzarella or Cheddar) 1/2 cup
Water 3 cups
Vegetables (optional: green onions, carrots) 1/2 cup (sliced)
Cooked chicken or tofu (optional) 1/2 cup

Step 1: Boil water in a pot and cook ramen noodles until tender. Drain most of the water, leaving about 1/4 cup.
Step 2: Stir in the Buldak spicy sauce with the noodles in the pot.
Step 3: Add vegetables and protein (optional) and stir well.
Step 4: Transfer the noodles to a serving bowl, sprinkle cheese on top, and let it melt.
Step 5: Garnish with green onions or sesame seeds if desired, and serve hot.

Beshbarmak

  • Time: 3 hours
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
Meat (lamb or beef) 1.5 kg
Flour 3 cups
Egg 1 large
Water 1/2 cup
Onions 3 large
Salt To taste

Step 1: Boil the meat with salt for 2-3 hours until tender. Remove and shred into pieces.
Step 2: Knead a firm dough with flour, water, egg, and salt. Roll it thin and cut it into squares.
Step 3: Boil the dough squares in the meat broth.
Step 4: Slice onions thinly and simmer them in the meat broth until soft.
Step 5: Serve the dough on a platter, topped with meat and onions. Pour some broth over it, and serve hot.

Manty

  • Time: 2 hours
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 3 cups
Egg 1 large
Water 1/2 cup
Minced meat (lamb or beef) 500 g
Onions 3 medium
Salt and pepper To taste

Step 1: Knead dough with flour, water, egg, and a pinch of salt. Let it rest for 30 minutes.
Step 2: Finely chop onions and mix with minced meat, salt, and pepper for the filling.
Step 3: Roll the dough thin, cut into squares, and add a spoonful of filling to each square.
Step 4: Fold and pinch the edges to seal, forming dumplings.
Step 5: Steam the Manty for about 45 minutes. Serve with sour cream or butter.
